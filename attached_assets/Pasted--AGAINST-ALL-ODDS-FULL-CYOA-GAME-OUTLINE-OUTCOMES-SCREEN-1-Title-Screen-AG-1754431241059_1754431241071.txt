## SCREEN 4: Debate Offer

**Narration:** Teacher invites Khalil to debate tryouts Friday after school.

### CHOICES:

* **Yes, sign up**
  *Outcome: Khalil begins researching topics and preparing speeches. His mom beams with pride when he tells her. Gains +Knowledge, opens path to scholarship, confidence boost.*
  *Quote: "Reality is wrong. Dreams are for real." — Tupac*

* **No, focus on work/family**
  *Outcome: Khalil doubles down on work and caregiving. He misses an opportunity but strengthens his bond with his siblings. Gains +Respect for family commitment, but closes debate path.*
  *Quote: "The biggest risk is not taking one." — Nipsey Hussle*

* **Try to do both**
  *Outcome: Khalil tries to prep for debate while working shifts. Sleep-deprived and stressed, he struggles to keep up. 50/50 chance to balance both. If successful, gains +Knowledge and +Money. If failed, loses both.*
  *Quote: "Success is my only option, failure's not." — Eminem*

## SCREEN 6: Street Offer

**Narration:** A friend offers Khalil a quick money gig: drop-offs for a local dealer.

### CHOICES:

* **Say NO**
  *Outcome: Keeps clean. Khalil's teacher notices his dedication. Gains +Respect and +Mental Strength. Trust with teachers improves.*
  *Quote: "I’d rather lose a leg than my integrity." — Logic*

* **Say YES**
  *Outcome: Gains +Money fast. Later risk of arrest or school trouble. Lowers +Respect. Opens risk path where he's approached again later.*
  *Quote: "More money, more problems." — The Notorious B.I.G.*

* **Walk away silently**
  *Outcome: No change immediately. Friend thinks Khalil's soft. Future trust path uncertain.*
